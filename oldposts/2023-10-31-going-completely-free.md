When I started this blog, I set out to share my stock and options research with the world. I started trading during the pandemic and read just about every book I could find on trading, options, and market psychology.

Fast forward to today and it's a hobby of mine that I really enjoy. There is always new data to process and new pathways to new predictions. I made this blog and all the posts free for everyone because getting started in the market involves losing money, especially when you first get started. 🤕

That's why I've removed all of the supporter plans from the site and cancelled existing plans! I'm incredibly thankful for the small group of people who contributed money so far and that's helped offset some of my hosting costs! **Thank you! **🫂 ♥️
![](__GHOST_URL__/content/images/2023/10/tenor-2226058573.gif)
**If you had a paid subscription before today,** I've cancelled that through Stripe and you shouldn't be billed again. Let me know if you run into any problems there. You'll still get my blog updates via email just like before.

**If you had a free subscription before today, nothing changes!** You still get my mediocre stock and options analysis in your inbox, just as you did before.
